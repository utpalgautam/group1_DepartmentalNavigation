## Quick Start

1. Select "Interactive Route" page
2. Choose building and floor
3. Use "Add Node" tab to place nodes
4. Use "Add Edge" tab to connect nodes
5. Click "Save Graph" to persist to Firebase
6. Switch between editors using toggle buttons
